Protocollege is a community-driven platform for generating proto-knowledge through proto-collage.

## Mission

Our mission is to create a space where protocols, case studies, and collaborative practices can be shared, refined, and built upon by the community.

## How It Works

- **Protocols** - Shared methods and practices
- **Case Studies** - Real-world applications and examples
- **Activities** - Community-driven projects
- **Blog** - Updates, reflections, and discussions
